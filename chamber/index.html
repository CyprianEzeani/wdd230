CITYVILLE AIM

The Cityville Chamber seeks to develop SA's first fully integrated business eco-system business chamber. We are providing an e-commerce platform to address the needs of consumer and entreprenuers to resolve challenges of market access, inter member trade and global opporunity creation. The Cityville CHAMBER OF COMMERCE is a membership-based association dedicated to creating an eco-system that provides access to markets, financial inclusion and socio-economic opportunities to the African, Small, Micro, Medium Enterprises. We are a collective voice for small businesses.
