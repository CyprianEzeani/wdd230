Overview

Purpose

The Cityville Chamber of Commerce serves two primary purposes:

  • Provide information about local businesses and services,
  • Facilitate business-to-business networking and promote local businesses.
  • Provide information on economic trends, community development, and public policies.

    • Scenarios

  • What upcoming events promote business networking in Cityville?
  • Where can I find contact information for the Chamber's board of directors?
  • What is the recent population growth in the Cityville area?
